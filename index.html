// How to Play

01

Objective

Destroy all 32 bricks on the board to win. Each brick awards 10 points.

02

Controls

Use the left and right arrow keys to slide the paddle. Keep the ball in play!

03

Scoring

+10 points per brick. Clear the entire board for a perfect 320-point run.

04

Game Over

If the ball falls past your paddle, it's game over. Your score is submitted automatically.
